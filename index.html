About me

Hey there! I'm a 22-year-old Software Engineer with a passion for creating awesome digital experiences. My coding journey started about 8 years ago when I first picked up Java, and I've been hooked ever since.

After diving into the Spigot API over 7 years ago, I began developing custom plugins and eventually turned this skill into a legitimate side hustle during 2020 when the world slowed down. What started as a hobby has successfully evolved into my career, and I couldn't be happier about it!

I'm comfortable with a range of development tools including Git, IntelliJ, and Gradle. My friends and colleagues know me as someone who's punctual, hard-working, and always gets the job done. My biggest strength? Problem-solving. I love tackling challenges head-on and finding creative solutions that work.
